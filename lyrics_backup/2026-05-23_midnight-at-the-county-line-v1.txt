[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
[Warm acoustic guitar strumming, intimate vocal presence]
I can feel your heartbeat through the steering wheel
Every single breath you take is all I want to steal

[Verse 1]
[Smooth electric guitar leads, soft acoustic texture]
The radio is whispering a slow and smoky tune

[Soft drum pocket with live human feel, melodic bass]

[Chorus]
[Big melodic central hook, smooth chorus lift, warm analog-style tone]
Oh, the city is a million miles away from what we have
Just the rhythm of our bodies down this lonely gravel path
I want to lose myself inside the warmth of your embrace
Let the midnight at the county line be our hiding place

[Post-Chorus]
[Subtle ambient pads]
[Background Vocal] (Oh, down at the county line)

[Acoustic-first emotional texture, intimate phrasing]

[Bridge]
[Spacious and emotionally close production, light piano accents]
